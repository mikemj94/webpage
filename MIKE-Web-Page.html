ABOUT DESTINY UNIVERSITY-SOUTH SUDAN.

Destiny University College Juba was founded in 2022. Destiny University originated from the Former Kampala University College Juba South Sudan and it had many Programs that are offered yearly. Destiny University have five Programs; Regular Programs, Evening Programs, Weekend Programs, Distance Learning Programs, Undergraduate Programs and Masters Programs for DUC.
